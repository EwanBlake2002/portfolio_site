About Me

Hello! I am an up and coming web developer graduate, garnering experience through projects put forward to me, and projects I undertake myself. My approach to designing websites puts the client front and centre, making use of the tools and creativity at my disposal.

I believe in the idea of thoughtful design - every element thought and overthought to ensure the best possible user experience for the people you want to attract. When I am not designing, crafting, or developing, you can find me hiking, experimenting with photography (Nature-related probably!), or exploring yet another cafes hot chocolate!
