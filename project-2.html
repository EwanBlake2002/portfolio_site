Organic ME

ORGANIC PRODUCTS AND SERVICES WEBSITE

Project Overview

Organic ME is a growing company based in the Borders region of Scotland, specializing in organic food products, household items, homeware, and clothing. They also produce their own organic honey, which is sold in their café, and organize monthly organic gardening courses. The company prides itself on selling high-quality, handmade products using locally sourced ingredients. Currently, Organic ME uses social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube for promotion but now seeks a dedicated website that is responsive and visually appealing across all devices. The website aims to attract more customers, increase profits, and promote tourism in the area.

Objectives

  • Create an attractive, professional website: The website must convey organic, quality, and outdoor themes.
  • Ensure accessibility and compliance: The website should be accessible to all users and comply with W3C standards.
  • Meet timeline and budget constraints: The website must be completed within 3 months and within the original budget of £1500.
  • Optimize for search engines: The website must be search engine friendly to improve visibility.
  • Design for usability and responsiveness: The website should be user-friendly and work on all modern browsers and devices.

Key Features

  • Price lists: Display prices for café and shop products.
  • Customer registration: Allow customers to register for monthly emails with offers and events.
  • Special offers: Advertise monthly special offers.
  • Product display: Show photos, prices, and details of organic products.
  • Contact information: Provide address, opening hours, and contact details.
  • Photo gallery: Display images of the organic gardening course and products.
  • Videos: Include videos of organic products for sale.
  • Google Map: Advertise the location using an embedded Google Map.
  • Social media integration: Link to at least one social media channel.

Methodology

  • User-centered design: Focus on the needs and preferences of the target audience.
  • Responsive web development: Ensure the website works seamlessly across all devices.
  • Search engine optimization (SEO): Optimize the website for better search engine visibility.
  • Usability testing: Conduct testing to ensure the website is easy to use and accessible to all users.

Key Features

Responsive Design

The web platform was developed to be easy to use on smaller devices - particularly mobiles and tablet devices.

Design Overhaul

Compared to the original design, there has been a significant overhaul to give it a more modern and sleek look.

Front-End Specific

The web platform is purely front-end built, but there is a future opportunity for a back-end to be integrated to add further interactivity.

Technologies Used
